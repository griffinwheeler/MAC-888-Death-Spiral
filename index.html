The Death Spiral — Peloton Case Study

Demand-Driven Death Spiral · Kenan-Flagler Business School · Cost Allocation Economics

The Death Spiral: As subscriber volume falls, Peloton's large fixed cost base (content studios, technology, R&D, SG&A) must be spread over fewer subscribers — driving up cost per subscriber. Higher costs erode competitiveness and accelerate further subscriber loss, repeating the cycle.

Formula: Unit Cost = Variable Cost + (Fixed Costs ÷ Volume). When Volume ↓, Unit Cost ↑ → margin collapses → further volume loss.
